How to Use Google

Using Google is very easy. You don't even have to be the greatest speller in the world.

All you have to have is a computer and an Internet connection. (Yeah, that's all.)

The following headings and images will guide you in conducting a search.

So brace yourself, here we go!!

Turn on your computer and connect to the Internet.

Type Google in the address bar. When the list of links

comes up click Google.

When the Google home page comes up,

type what you want to search for in the text box

located at the middle of the page.

You can type anything. Let's try searching for trains.

Finding information using Google is very easy. As described above

all you have to do is type in the subject, name, place, or item you want

information on. Millions of links will come up for you to explore.

Let's say you want to go directly to a specific page. I will create a

link for you so you can go to Amtrak's website.
